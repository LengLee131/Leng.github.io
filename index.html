About Us

Step into Lee Restaurant and discover a warm, inviting space where tradition meets taste. Located in the heart of Milwaukee’s HmongTown Marketplace, we offer a menu crafted with bold, comforting flavors that represent the heart of Hmong cooking. From aromatic broths to rich sauces, every dish tells a story—one of family, heritage, and community.

Lee Restaurant is more than just a place to eat—it’s a gathering spot for neighbors, families, and first-time visitors alike. We’re proud to serve a wide variety of traditional dishes, including pho, sticky rice, crispy pork belly, and other homestyle favorites that celebrate the richness of our culture.

Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a family dinner, you’ll be welcomed with friendly service, generous portions, and the kind of flavor that brings people together. Join us and experience Hmong culture through food—fresh, soulful, and made with love.
